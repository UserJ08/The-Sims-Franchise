The Sims is a franchise of life simulation games developed by Maxis
and published by Electronic Arts. The Sims has nearly 200 million copies sold worldwide.
Sims games are spread on most platforms - computers, laptops, console and mobile devices.
The franchise consists of sandbox games, where you create a sim, place them in a house
and play as you wish.

Such games have no set objective or end, therefore you can play as long as you like.
The franchise started with SimsCity in 1989.
